Dog Meat Soup

Mouth Watering Soup you'll taste

  • 100g of boiled dog meat- In a hot water boil a freshly skinned dog. Cut the dog into pieces and measure 100g from it.
  • 20g of green onion- Can use either a white or pink onion depending on the amount of spiciness of your preference.
  • 8g of salt- White refined salt is better than an sea salt.
  • 2g of mashed gralic-Using stone mash garlic in table.
  • 500g of gravy
  • 2g of red pepper-Green papper can be made ripe by storing them at table counter.

How to cook the soup?

  1. After boiling the meat with gravy and stalk of taro for some time, boil again after putting vegetables and other ingredients into it.
  2. Before eating, sprinkle pepper on it and put into an earthen bowl.
  3. The stalk of taro is to be kept in cold water one or two days to get rid of its smell and taste.
