What is cricket?

A sport played by two teams of 11 players.

One might mistake cricket as some other things other than a sport. There are various meanings of cricket. Many people are familiar with cricket as being an insect or wireless cricket phone. Cricket Wireless was founded in 1999, the sport of cricket was created much before that.

Cricket is a sport played mainly in south asia. It was developed in England which became popular in India and other countries under British colony. It is the second most popular sport in the world behind soccer. 2.5 billion people watched the cricket WC 2019 held in England.
Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a field at the centre of which is a 22-yard (20-metre) pitch with a wicket at each end, each comprising two bails balanced on three stumps. The batting side scores runs by striking the ball bowled at one of the wickets with the bat and then running between the wickets, while the bowling and fielding side tries to prevent this (by preventing the ball from leaving the field, and getting the ball to either wicket) and dismiss each batter (so they are "out").

There are mainly three forms of cricket. Test, One Day and Twenty overs. Test cricket lasts for five days, while one day lasts for an average of eight hours & twenty twenty lasts for three and half hours. Different leagues are played around the world in T20(twenty twenty) format. The most prominent league is IPL—Indian Premier League.
